Description of Site

This app allows you to create a catalog of your favorite recipes by inputting the recipe URL into the Add a New Recipe option in the lower-left corner of the homepage and submitting them into the database. Once your recipes have been loaded into the database, you can let the app choose which recipe you should cook.

While the app does the hard work of picking which recipe to make, you still have some say in what food gets made. Using the restrictions available on the left side of the screen, the user can tell the app what type of food they want:

  • Recipe Type lets you pick what type of dish they want to cook. Want to make a breakfast dish for a weekend brunch, the perfect after-dinner dessert, or an appetizer to snack on? Choose the applicable option from this menu.
  • Whether it's a pasta dish from Italy, a curry from India, or Mexican street food, if you've ever had a craving for a specific type of food, the Cuisine menu lets you pick the genre of food you'll be eating tonight.
  • If someone you're cooking for has dietary restrictions, food allergies, or is just a picky eater, the Foods to Avoid menu helps you make sure that your next meal can be enjoyed by all.

Once you've picked what general type of food you'd like to make, click on the Generate! button and the app will pick which meal you should make.
